Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your monthly or annual plan at any time from your account dashboard. You will retain access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing period.

What happens if I use all my credits?

If you run out of credits before your plan renews, you can easily purchase a top-up credit pack to continue your work without interruption. Annual plan members get a special discount on top-up packs.

Do unused credits roll over?

Monthly credits do not roll over and will reset at the start of each billing cycle. This allows us to maintain our server capacity and offer competitive pricing.
