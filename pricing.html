How Credits Work

We use a simple Credit system to ensure fair resource allocation:

  • 1 Credit: Used for Bulk Download of 1 YouTube URL (Official Subtitles only).
  • 5 - 10 Credits: Used for AI Transcription of 1 YouTube URL (Reflects external processing costs).
  • All Free Accounts receive 5 Credits/day, which reset every 24 hours.
