About YTVidHub

Building a faster, simpler, and more powerful tool for the creative community.

Our Mission

YTVidHub was born from a simple frustration: downloading subtitles from multiple YouTube videos was a slow, repetitive, and inefficient process. As content creators, researchers, and language learners ourselves, we spent countless hours copying, pasting, and downloading files one by one. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our mission is to provide the most efficient and user-friendly YouTube subtitle downloader on the web. We are committed to three core principles:

  • Efficiency First: Our signature bulk download feature is the heart of our platform. We believe in building tools that respect your time and accelerate your workflow.
  • Free and Accessible for Everyone: Powerful tools shouldn't come with a price tag. YTVidHub is, and always will be, completely free to use without any limitations or hidden fees.
  • Privacy by Design: We believe in your right to privacy. Our service is built to be anonymous and secure. We do not track you, and we do not store the URLs you enter or the files you download.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

YTVidHub ("we," "us," or "our") is fundamentally committed to user privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning information. Simply put, we are designed from the ground up to collect as little information as possible.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We believe the most secure user data is no data at all. Therefore, we DO NOT collect, store, or log any of the following:

  • The YouTube URLs you enter into our tool.
  • The subtitle files (SRT or TXT) that you download.
  • Your IP address or any other personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Any tracking data that links your identity to your usage of the tool.

The entire subtitle fetching and processing is done on-the-fly. Once your download is complete, the session's data is gone.

3. Information We Do Collect (Anonymous Analytics)

To maintain and improve our service, we use a privacy-focused analytics platform (like Fathom or a self-hosted solution) that does not use cookies and does not track individuals. We may collect anonymous, aggregated data, such as:

  • The total number of downloads per day.
  • The general geographic region of our users (e.g., "United States," not "New York City").
  • The type of browser used (e.g., "Chrome," not a specific user's browser fingerprint).

This data is not and cannot be tied to an individual user. It helps us understand traffic patterns and make the service better.

4. Cookies

YTVidHub does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. The core functionality of our website does not require cookies to operate.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service interacts directly with YouTube's servers to fetch publicly available subtitle data. Our use of this data is transient and is governed by YouTube's Terms of Service. We do not share any data with other third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@ytvidhub.com.
