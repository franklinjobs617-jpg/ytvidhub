About YTVidHub

Building a faster, simpler, and more powerful tool for the creative community.

Our Mission

YTVidHub was born from a simple frustration: downloading subtitles from multiple YouTube videos was a slow, repetitive, and inefficient process. As content creators, researchers, and language learners ourselves, we spent countless hours copying, pasting, and downloading files one by one. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our mission is to provide the most efficient and user-friendly YouTube subtitle downloader on the web. We are committed to three core principles:

  • Efficiency First: Our signature bulk download feature is the heart of our platform. We believe in building tools that respect your time and accelerate your workflow.
  • Free and Accessible for Everyone: We offer a generous free tier for everyday use. For power users, we provide premium options that ensure the long-term sustainability and improvement of our service.
  • Privacy by Design: We believe in your right to privacy. Our service is built to be anonymous and secure. We do not track you, and we do not store the URLs you enter or the files you download.
