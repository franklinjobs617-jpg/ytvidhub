# SEO Backlink and Brand Mention Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-22

## Goal

Build the external trust signals needed to move YTVidHub core pages from positions 8-18 toward Top 5 for YouTube subtitle downloader queries.

## Anchor Text Rules

- Prefer branded anchors: `YTVidHub`, `ytvidhub.com`, `YTVidHub subtitle downloader`.
- Use natural anchors: `YouTube subtitle tool`, `subtitle download workflow`, `caption export tool`.
- Avoid repeating exact-match anchors such as `youtube subtitle downloader` more than a few times.

## First 30 Days

1. Submit to 10 AI/tool directories.
2. Submit to 5 video, creator, or subtitle-related directories.
3. Add links from Chrome extension listing and extension documentation.
4. Publish or update 3 GitHub/README mentions where relevant.
5. Create 3 helpful community posts or comments on Reddit, Quora, or creator forums.
6. Publish 2 short YouTube/tutorial references showing how to download SRT/VTT/TXT with YTVidHub.

## Quality Checklist

- The linking page should be indexed or realistically indexable.
- The mention should describe the actual product, not just drop a link.
- Avoid automated mass submissions, PBNs, spun articles, and paid exact-match anchors.
- Track every submission with date, URL, target page, anchor text, and status.
