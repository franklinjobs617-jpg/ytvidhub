Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not just a policy, it's our core design philosophy.

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to YTVidHub ("we", "our", or "us"). We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains our principles and practices regarding information. Our guiding rule is simple: we collect the absolute minimum amount of information required to provide our service, and we have architected our system to be anonymous by design.

2. The Information We Intentionally Do Not Collect

We believe the most secure user data is the data you never collect. Therefore, our service is built to be stateless and anonymous. We explicitly DO NOT collect, log, or store any of the following personal or sensitive information:

  • Your IP Address: Your IP address is not logged or stored on our servers in connection with your activity.
  • Submitted URLs: The YouTube video URLs you paste into our tool are used in real-time to fetch subtitles and are immediately discarded. They are never written to a database or log file.
  • Downloaded Files: The subtitle files (SRT or TXT) you download are generated on-the-fly and streamed directly to you. They never touch our hard drives.
  • Personal Information: Since we do not have an account system, we do not ask for, nor do we store, your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. Anonymous and Aggregated Usage Data

To operate and improve YTVidHub, we need to understand basic traffic patterns. For this, we use a privacy-centric web analytics service that does not track individual users. The data we collect is anonymous, aggregated, and cannot be used to identify you. This includes:

  • Total Page Views: To understand which pages are most popular.
  • General Geographic Data: We may know that a user is from the United States, but not their city or specific location.
  • Referrer Information: To know if you came from a Google search or another website.
  • Device and Browser Type: To ensure our website functions correctly on different screen sizes and in different browsers (e.g., "Chrome on Desktop").

This data helps us answer questions like "How many people used the bulk download feature today?" not "What videos did User X download?".

4. Our Stance on Cookies

YTVidHub does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling purposes. The core functionality of our service does not require any cookies. Any essential cookies used would be strictly for basic site functionality and would not contain personal information.

5. Third-Party Interactions

Our tool interacts with YouTube's public infrastructure to retrieve publicly available subtitle data. This process is done server-side to protect your privacy. Your browser only communicates with our servers, not directly with YouTube's servers through our tool. We do not share any data with any other third parties.

6. Data Security

While we do not store your personal data, we take the security of our platform seriously. We employ standard security practices, including HTTPS for all communications, to ensure that your interaction with our site is secure and encrypted, protecting it from eavesdropping.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy in the future to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always indicate when the last changes were made.

8. How to Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are always happy to clarify our position on privacy. You can reach us at:
