Your Bulk Downloading Questions Answered

Is there a limit to how many URLs I can paste at once?

While our system is designed to handle a large number of URLs, we recommend processing up to 50 URLs at a time for optimal performance. This ensures a fast and smooth experience without browser timeouts.

How are the files named inside the ZIP archive?

To keep things organized, each subtitle file inside the ZIP archive is named after the original YouTube video's title. This makes it incredibly easy to match each subtitle file to its corresponding video.

Can I download transcripts in bulk as .TXT files?

Yes! Our bulk downloader supports both SRT and TXT formats. You can choose your preferred format before initiating the download, and all files in the resulting ZIP archive will be in that format.
