Frequently Asked Questions

Does YTVidHub handle all languages in a playlist?

Yes. Our bulk downloader detects all available languages (both original and auto-generated) for every video in the playlist, allowing you to select the required language before you download.

Can I download subtitles for a whole YouTube Channel?

Our Bulk Engine is optimized for Playlist URLs. To download all videos from a Channel, simply place the Channel's videos into a temporary (or permanent) playlist on YouTube and use our tool with that playlist URL.
